# Ursuline AI Lesson Package Overview

Two-part lesson series for Ursuline Academy.

## Part 1 · AI Literacy
**AI, the Brain, and Serviam** · grades 7–12 · about 20 minutes

Covers AI literacy, brain science, prompt-to-response mechanics, SERVIAM, and judicious AI use.

## Part 2 · Inside AI
**The Math, Science, and Code Behind Intelligent Tools** · grades 10–11 · about 15–20 minutes

Extends Part 1 with functions, tokens, vectors, weights, loss, probability, attention, generation loops, testing, and human review.

## How the parts fit together
Submit Part 1 as the core student lesson. Offer Part 2 as a supplemental extension for upper-school CS, math, science, and AP readiness.

## Student-facing site
The lesson website presents student interactives, slide embeds, and handouts at the top. Team and leadership materials live in the section at the bottom of the same page.
