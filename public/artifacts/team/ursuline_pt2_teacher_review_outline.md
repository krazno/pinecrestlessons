# Teacher Review Outline
## Inside AI: The Math, Science, and Code Behind Intelligent Tools

Purpose: Leader-facing companion for Part 2. It explains why the supplemental lesson fits Ursuline, how it extends Part 1, and how it supports upper-school STEM.

## 1. Why Part 2 exists
Part 1 covers introduction to AI, internal workings at a high level, and judicious use. Part 2 scales that lesson for grades 10 and 11 into a concise math, science, and CS extension.

## 2. Core thesis
The model can produce language. The learner must still question, verify, explain, and decide.

## 3. How it extends Part 1
Part 1 uses the pathway prompt, tokens, numbers, attention, probability, response, and human review. Part 2 expands the middle with functions, vectors, weights, loss, softmax, attention, and a short generation loop.

## 4. Ursuline fit
The lesson connects technical understanding to Serviam, intellectual curiosity, integrity, purpose, presence, the I.D.E.A. Hub, and young women as thoughtful builders and evaluators of technology.

## 5. Student experience
Students predict, connect a model to a function, map tokens to vectors, compare similarity, interpret a weighted equation, describe learning from error, read pseudocode, and close with an exit ticket.

## 6. Age-appropriate design
Formulas are visual anchors, not computation-heavy tasks. The lesson targets strong 10th and 11th graders, with optional AP extension language in teacher notes.

## 7. Pedagogy embedded
Hook and prior knowledge activation, short teacher modeling, diagram-first explanations, visible thinking questions, student talk, quick checks, transfer to science, math, CS, and ethics, and a metacognitive exit ticket.

## 8. Standards and frameworks
Aligned to AP CSP, AP CSA, CSTA AI Learning Priorities, AI4K12, Google ML foundations, NIST trustworthy AI characteristics, and UNESCO student AI competency guidance.

## 9. Recommendation
Offer Part 1 as the core lesson. Include Part 2 as an optional supplement that demonstrates depth for upper-school STEM and shows how the lesson scales.
